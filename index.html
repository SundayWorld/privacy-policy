Privacy Policy & Data Safety

Effective Date: December 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications created and published by Daodu Sunday, including:

  • AII Document Viewer
  • Music Player
  • Ultra Phone Cleaner
  • Quick PDF Converter
  • Quick Math Solver (AI)
  • My 3D PDF Library
  • Homework Helper AI
  • QuickTranslate AI

1. Information We Collect

Most of our apps do not collect or store any personal data. All data stays on your device unless stated otherwise.

For apps using AI (Quick Math Solver, Homework Helper AI, QuickTranslate AI):

  • We do not store user queries.
  • Our backend only stores your API key securely so the app can function.
  • No personal information is collected or saved.

For non-AI apps:

These apps operate fully offline and do not collect any personal information.

  • Music Player: Accesses audio files only on your device.
  • Quick PDF Converter / AII Document Viewer: Files are processed locally. No upload happens.
  • Ultra Phone Cleaner: Cleans cache and junk locally on the device.
  • My 3D PDF Library: Loads local PDF files; nothing is uploaded.

2. Data We Never Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • Location
  • Photos or media (except the ones you select for the app to process)
  • Payment information
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising ID

No data is sold, shared, or tracked.

Internet
